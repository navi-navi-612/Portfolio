VENDRA NAVEENA

Fashion Designer | Sustainable & Avant-Garde Fashion

Creativity and sustainability drive my work as a fashion designer. Inspired by nature and modern art, I create innovative and ethically produced garments that merge aesthetics with functionality. My expertise lies in draping, textile experimentation, and digital fashion design.

A Bit About Me

I’m Naveena, a fashion designer with a background in Information Technology. My journey started in the world of technology, but my passion for creativity and aesthetics led me to explore the dynamic world of fashion design. Blending my technical expertise with artistic vision, I specialize in sustainable fashion, textile innovation, digital fashion design. My designs reflect a fusion of structure and fluidity, influenced by my engineering mindset and love for innovation. Transitioning from engineering to fashion, I’ve embraced my passion for design, specializing in modern silhouettes, fabric technology, and eco-friendly fashion. My work is driven by a blend of precision and creativity, ensuring every piece is both aesthetically compelling and structurally sound.
